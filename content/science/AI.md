# AI in Astronomy

Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing how we study the universe.

***

## Detecting Exoplanets

AI helps astronomers sift through massive datasets to find patterns indicative of exoplanets.

***

## Classifying Galaxies

Machine learning algorithms can classify galaxies by shape much faster than humans can.
